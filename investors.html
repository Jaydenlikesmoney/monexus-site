A Platform, Not a Product

One living technology, a multi-disease pipeline, defensible platform IP, and a staged path from preclinical toward the clinic.

Market

Antimicrobial resistance, chronic wounds, device infection, and oral disease — large, underserved, and converging on the same unmet need: the failure of burst-dose therapies. Together these conditions represent approximately US$1.5 trillion in annual global economic burden¹ — roughly US$700B from drug-resistant infections², US$710B from oral disease³, and US$149B in chronic-wound care⁴.

¹ Approximate sum of the figures below; categories partly overlap. ² WHO / BMJ Global Health (2025). ³ Journal of Dental Research (2024). ⁴ Advances in Wound Care (2025).
